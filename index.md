  # VaderBot

  VaderBot is a personal automation assistant that connects to Google
  services (Gmail, YouTube, Calendar, Drive) to help you manage your
  accounts through natural language commands.

  ## Features

  - Email management and inbox automation via Gmail
  - YouTube content uploading, scheduling, and analytics
  - Calendar and task management
  - AI-powered content generation for titles, descriptions, and more
