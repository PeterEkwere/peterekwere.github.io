 # Vader Privacy Policy                                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                                  
  Last updated: February 26, 2026                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                  
  ## Overview                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                  
  Vader is a personal automation tool that integrates with Google services                                                                        
  (Gmail, YouTube, Calendar, Drive, and others) to perform actions on your
  behalf. This policy explains how your data is handled.

  ## Data Collection and Usage

  - Vader accesses your Google account data solely to perform actions you
    explicitly request (reading emails, uploading videos, managing calendar, etc.)
  - All data processing happens locally on your own machine
  - No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers
  - No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties
  - No analytics or tracking is performed on your data

  ## Data Storage

  - OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device for persistent access
  - No copies of your emails, videos, or other content are retained beyond
    what is needed to complete the requested action
  - You can delete all locally stored data at any time

  ## Third-Party Services

  - Vader uses Google APIs (Gmail API, YouTube Data API, etc.) to interact
    with your Google account
  - Vader may use AI language models (via OpenRouter) to generate text
    content such as email drafts, video titles, and descriptions. No
    personal data from your Google account is sent to these AI services
    without your explicit instruction.

  ## Your Rights

  - You can revoke Vader's access at any time via
    https://myaccount.google.com/permissions
  - You can request deletion of all locally stored data
  - You retain full ownership and control of your data at all times

  ## Security

  - All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  - OAuth 2.0 is used for authentication — Vader never sees or stores your
    Google password
  - Tokens are stored with file-system-level permissions restricted to your
    user account

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by
  the "Last updated" date above.
